I really like both The Thing and Alien as movie concepts. Alien presents a lot of interesting satire by pointing out the flaws
of a capitalist society and how it would affect the human population when it comes to a living bioweapon.
The Thing is just a very well structured movie. It waits until your guard is down to showcase the creature and the plot then moves forward from there.
